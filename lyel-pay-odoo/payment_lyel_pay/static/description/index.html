Lyel Pay for Odoo

Accept mobile money and card payments across Central and West Africa, directly from your Odoo checkout.

Why Lyel Pay

  • Mobile money native. Airtel Money, Orange Money, MTN Mobile Money — the payment methods your customers actually use.
  • Multi-currency. XAF, XOF, CDF, EUR and USD out of the box.
  • Embedded checkout. A hosted iframe keeps your customer inside your store while staying PCI-compliant.
  • Webhook-driven. Authoritative transaction updates, so your orders are never out of sync with payment reality.

What this module does

  • Adds Lyel Pay to your Payment Providers settings.
  • Enables Lyel Pay at the website checkout.
  • Adds a "Pay Now" link to invoices, payable via mobile money.
  • Listens for Lyel Pay webhooks and updates Odoo transactions automatically.

Configuration

  1. Install the module.
  2. Open Settings → Payment Providers → Lyel Pay.
  3. Set state to Test or Enabled.
  4. Paste your publishable key, secret key and webhook secret from the Lyel Pay dashboard.
  5. Save. Configure the webhook URL on the dashboard: https://your-odoo-domain/payment/lyel_pay/webhook.

Compatibility

Odoo 17 & Odoo 18. Community and Enterprise.
